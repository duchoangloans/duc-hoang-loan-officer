Loan Programs

4 Ways to Qualify Without Traditional Income Docs

During your free consultation, I'll recommend which program fits your situation best.

01

Bank Statement Loans

12 or 24-Month Statements

We review your personal or business bank deposits over the last 12–24 months to calculate your qualifying income. No tax returns. No W-2s. No pay stubs.

  • Personal or business bank statements accepted
  • 12-month or 24-month options available
  • Monthly deposits averaged to determine income
  • Perfect for self-employed & cash-heavy businesses
  • Loan amounts up to $3M
Ideal for: Self-employed, business owners, freelancers, gig workers
Ask Me About This Program
02

Profit & Loss Statement Loans

CPA or Self-Prepared P&L

Use a Profit & Loss statement prepared by you or your CPA as proof of income. One of the most flexible options for business owners with strong revenue.

  • Borrower-prepared or CPA-prepared accepted
  • Shows revenue minus expenses = net income
  • No bank statements required in some cases
  • Quick and simple documentation
  • Great for newer businesses
Ideal for: Business owners, independent contractors, sole proprietors
Ask Me About This Program
03

VOE Loan Program

Verification of Employment

Your employer provides a written verification of your employment and income directly. No pay stubs, no tax returns — just a simple letter from your employer.

  • Employer verifies position, tenure, and income
  • No pay stubs or tax returns needed
  • Works for cash-based employees
  • Simple, fast process
  • Available for primary residences
Ideal for: Cash-paid employees, workers without pay stubs, tipped workers
Ask Me About This Program
04

DSCR Investor Loans

Debt Service Coverage Ratio

For real estate investors — qualify based on the property's rental income, not your personal income. If the rent covers the mortgage, you can qualify.

  • No personal income verification at all
  • Qualify based on property cash flow
  • Rental income ÷ mortgage payment = DSCR
  • Investment properties & short-term rentals
  • Close in LLC or personal name
Ideal for: Real estate investors, landlords, Airbnb hosts, portfolio builders

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll have a quick 15-minute phone call where I learn about your income situation, the type of property you're looking for, and your budget. Then I'll tell you which loan program fits best and give you an estimated monthly payment and closing costs — all before any paperwork.

Do I really not need to submit documents upfront?

Correct. I'll provide your estimates based on our conversation. You only submit bank statements, P&L, or other documents after you've reviewed the numbers and decided you want to move forward.

Will you pull my credit during the consultation?

No. I won't pull your credit until you give me the green light to proceed. The consultation is completely non-committal.

Do I really not need tax returns to get a mortgage?

Correct. Our bank statement, P&L, and VOE programs do not require tax returns. We use alternative documentation to verify your income and ability to repay.

What credit score do I need?

Minimum credit scores vary by program but generally start around 620. The higher your score, the better your rate and terms. We'll discuss this on our call.

How much down payment is required?

Most non-QM programs require 10–20% down depending on the loan amount and credit profile. Some programs may offer options with as little as 10% down. I'll give you the exact number during our call.

What's the difference between a bank statement loan and a P&L loan?

A bank statement loan uses 12–24 months of actual bank deposits to calculate income. A P&L loan uses a Profit & Loss statement showing business revenue and expenses. Both avoid tax returns entirely. I'll recommend which one is better for you.

Can I use a DSCR loan for Airbnb or short-term rentals?

Absolutely. DSCR loans work great for short-term rentals and Airbnb properties. We use the projected rental income to qualify — your personal income isn't a factor.

How fast can I close?

Most non-QM loans close in 21–30 days, sometimes faster depending on appraisal timelines and documentation readiness.
