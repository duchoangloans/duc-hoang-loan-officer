About Me

Duc Hoang

Loan Officer | 24-7 Mortgage

I'm Duc Hoang, a dedicated Loan Officer specializing in helping first-time homebuyers turn their dream of homeownership into reality. I understand that navigating the mortgage process can feel overwhelming — especially if you're self-employed, have non-traditional income, or aren't sure how much you need for a down payment.

That's why I focus on creative financing solutions that many other lenders overlook. From stated income programs that don't require traditional pay stubs or W-2s, to down payment assistance programs that can get you into a home with as little as nothing down, I work tirelessly to find the right loan for your unique situation.

Loan Programs

Financing Solutions Tailored to You

Whether you're buying your first home or investing in property, I offer specialized programs to fit your needs.

Conventional Loans

Traditional financing with competitive rates. Ideal for borrowers with established credit and income documentation.

  • As low as 5% down payment
  • Fixed & adjustable rates
  • Primary & investment properties
Specialty

Stated Income / No Doc

Self-employed or non-traditional income? No W-2s or tax returns needed. Qualify based on bank statements or asset verification.

  • No tax returns required
  • Bank statement programs
  • Perfect for self-employed

Down Payment Assistance

Programs that help cover your down payment and closing costs so you can get into a home sooner.

  • Grants & forgivable loans
  • Up to 100% financing
  • First-time buyer programs

Hard Money Loans

Fast, asset-based lending for investors and time-sensitive deals. Close in as little as 7 days.

  • Quick closings
  • Asset-based approval
  • Fix & flip friendly

5% Down Programs

Get into your home with minimal money down. Designed for buyers who want to preserve cash while building equity.

  • Only 5% down required
  • Competitive interest rates
  • No PMI options available

FHA Loans

Government-backed loans with flexible qualification criteria. Great for first-time buyers with lower credit scores.

  • As low as 3.5% down
  • Lower credit score OK
  • Gift funds allowed
